Rethinking College Admissions

Advocating for a More Supportive, Transparent, and Equitable Process

The Problem

Over the past decade, the number of college applications per student has increased dramatically. Students are applying to more schools due to heightened competition, resulting in application inflation and rising stress levels.

Source: Common App Data 2023

Acceptance Rates Are Falling

Despite record-high application numbers, the number of available spots at top institutions has remained relatively stable, causing acceptance rates to plummet and further fueling the arms race of extracurriculars and test scores.

Source: Ivy Coach / Institutional Reports

What Students Need

  • ✅ Clearer guidance from school counselors
  • ✅ Better access to affordable college advising resources
  • ✅ Transparency around admissions criteria
  • ✅ Mental health support during application season
